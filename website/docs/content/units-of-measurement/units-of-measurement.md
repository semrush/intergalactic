---
title: Units of measurement
---

* Omit a space between a number and a single letter or symbol
* Use “K” for thousands, “M” for millions, “B” for billions
* Use “s” for seconds, “m” for minutes, “h” for hours
* Use uppercase for time

<DosDonts>
    <template #dont>
        <p>10 K</p>
        <p>3 s</p>
        <p>9:45 a.m.</p>
    </template>
    <template #do>
        <p>10K</p>
        <p>3s</p>
        <p>9:45 AM</p>
    </template>
</DosDonts>
